Privacy Policy for Biased Flip

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Biased Flip ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Biased Flip is a simple game that does not require user accounts or collect personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information to provide and improve our services.

Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Location data (general, not precise)
  • Interactions with advertisements

This information is used to provide personalized ads and measure ad performance. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Data

We do not collect or transmit any game data (scores, progress, etc.) to our servers. All game data is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Analyze app performance and fix issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Google AdMob - For advertising services. AdMob's use of information is governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • Service Providers - Who help us operate our app (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements - When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized ads - You can disable personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID)
  • Request information - You can request information about data we collect
  • Request deletion - You can request deletion of your data
  • Uninstall the app - You can uninstall the app at any time, which will remove locally stored data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.
